# gentle Stretch Educational Materials

This folder contains AIML's gentle Stretch / Brain-Healthy Activity Planner handout.

## File

- `gentle_stretch_Handout.pdf` — four-page handout for participants.

## What it covers

The handout includes:

1. **Brain-Healthy Activity Planner** — a simple goal of about 750 MET points per week, with Gentle Stretch Wellness class examples and helpful add-on activities such as walking, cycling, swimming, and water exercise.
2. **Build Your 750-Point Week** — a weekly worksheet for choosing activities, entering MET points, and totaling the week.
3. **Brain-Healthy Nutrition Handout** — a MIND-style eating guide plus a daily multivitamin note.
4. **Cognitive Engagement + Local Resources** — practical examples of cognitive engagement and Western North Carolina resources.

## Audience

Older adults, community wellness participants, facilitators, and local partners interested in practical brain-health education.

## Notes for facilitators

This material is educational and should not be presented as medical advice. Participants should be encouraged to talk with their doctor about blood pressure, diabetes, smoking, cholesterol, obesity, sleep apnea, and other health risks that affect brain health.
