# gentle Stretch / Brain-Healthy Activity Planner

This AIML handout helps participants plan a brain-healthy week using a simple 750 MET-point goal.

The handout includes movement planning, a weekly activity worksheet, MIND-style nutrition guidance, and cognitive engagement resources for Western North Carolina.

[Download the PDF](../materials/gentle-stretch/gentle_stretch_Handout.pdf)

## Highlights

- Gentle Stretch Wellness classes and add-on activities
- Weekly planner for building a 750-point activity week
- MIND-style nutrition guide
- Cognitive engagement examples and local resources

## Medical note

This material is educational. Participants should talk with their doctor about health conditions and risks such as blood pressure, diabetes, smoking, cholesterol, obesity, and sleep apnea.
